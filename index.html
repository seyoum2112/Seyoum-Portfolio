Seyoum Nigussie, Lecturer in the Department of Electromechanical Engineering at Addis Ababa Science and Technology University.

Over 4 years of teaching and research experience focused on improving engineering education through hands-on learning. My work combines analytical and experimental methods to solve real-world energy and design challenges.
